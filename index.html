About us

Welcome to cat-model

CAT model is a web-based platform for evaluating the risk to a portfolio through exposure to natural perils. Our models are constructed at a detailed level and then aggregated up to match standard exposure zones, typically Cresta zones. Our focus is on insurance and reinsurance companies, (re)insurance brokers and other stakeholders actively involved with property risk assessment.

Services we offer

Portfolio modeling

Model alternative portfolios for multiple countries and perils.

Reinsurance optimisation

Test alternative reinsurances using either pre-set options or more unique programmes.

Insurance

Our focus is on insurance, reinsurance, (re)insurance brokers and other stakeholders with financial exposure to natural catastrophes.

On-line access to load exposure profiles

Our clients have online access to load their exposure profiles and model the exposures in different countries against the different modeled perils.

Results in the form of easily Interpretable maps

Our focus is on presenting results in ways which are clear and in-line with common practice, such as return period losses and OEP / AEP curves.

Solvency and Capital reviews

Optimize your capital requirements by minimizing your CAT charge.
